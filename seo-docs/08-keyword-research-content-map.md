## PART 3: COMPETITOR CONTENT ANALYSIS

### 3.1 Top 5 Competitors in French Rental Property Management SEO Space

#### Competitor 1: **SeLoger** (seloger.com)
- **Authority:** DR 87, ~42M monthly visits
- **Strengths:** Massive content library, real estate portal with deep domain authority, strong local SEO
- **Weaknesses:** Complex navigation, no free tools/templates, high competition keywords
- **Top Ranking Keywords:** gestion locative, estimation loyer, annonce location
- **Content Type:** Mostly informational + listings; limited free tools
- **Content Gaps We Can Exploit:**
  - Free downloadable templates (SeLoger doesn't offer these)
  - Interactive calculators without registration
  - Plain-language legal guides (SeLoger is overly professional/formal)
  - Step-by-step procedural content (checklist format)
