## 4. Programmatic SEO Page Families

Programmatic pages are generated from structured data with real user value. Each family follows anti-thin-content rules.

### Page Family 1: City Pages
**Pattern:** `/gestion-locative/{ville}`
**Scope:** 50+ French cities
**Data source:** `cities.json` with population, rental market data
**Anti-thin-content rules:**
- Unique intro paragraph per city
- City-specific rental statistics (avg rent, vacancy rate)
- Local regulation notes (encadrement applies?)
- 3 local blog article links
- Internal linking to relevant tools
- Minimum 400 words of unique city-specific content

|-------|--------|

## 5. Internal Linking Strategy Outline

*(Full version in `seo-docs/00-master-content-strategy.md` Section C)*

### Hub-and-Spoke Model

```
HOMEPAGE (authority hub)
    │
    ├── Feature Hub (/gestion-locative) ────► Tool pages
    │                                        ────► Blog articles
    │                                        ────► City pages
    │
    ├── Blog Hub (/blog) ────────────────────► Pillar pages
    │                                        ────► Tool pages
    │                                        ────► Glossary
    │
    ├── Tool Hub (/outils) ──────────────────► Blog articles
    │                                        ────► Glossary terms
    │                                        ────► /register
    │
    └── Glossary Hub (/glossaire-immobilier) ──► Blog articles
                                                ──► Tool pages
```

### Authority Flow Rules
1. Homepage passes most PageRank to: /pricing, /quittances, /blog
2. Feature pages act as secondary hubs, linking to their respective tool + blog content
3. Blog articles: min 3 contextual links (1 to tool, 1 to another article, 1 to glossary)
4. Tool pages: min 2 links to blog explainers + 1 CTA to /register
5. City pages: link to city-specific blog content + tool pages
6. Glossary: each term links to most relevant tool/blog page

### Anchor Text Principles
- Descriptive anchors with keywords: "générez vos quittances PDF"
- Avoid: "cliquez ici", "en savoir plus", bare URLs
- Product CTAs: "Essayez gratuitement", "Commencez votre essai"
