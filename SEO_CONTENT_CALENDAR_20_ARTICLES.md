## SEO Brief Template (for writers)

```
## SEO BRIEF — [ARTICLE TITLE]

### Métadonnées
- **Slug** : [generated-from-title]
- **Primary keyword** : [exact target keyword]
- **Secondary keywords** : [3-5 LSI variants]
- **Search intent** : [informational / commercial / transactional]
- **Word count target** : [1500-2500]
- **FAQ** : [3-5 Q&A pairs — add FAQPage schema]

### Structure obligatoire
1. **H1** : [Exact match title with year if relevant]
2. **Intro** (100-150 mots) : Hook + search intent match + article roadmap
3. **Section H2** : [Step-by-step ou thématique]
   - H3 subsections as needed
4. **Section H2** : [Continuing content]
5. **Tableau comparatif** (if applicable)
6. **Encadré prático** (callout/tip box)
7. **FAQ Section** (5 questions minimum)
8. **CTA** : Lien vers outil/template pertinent

### E-E-A-T Requirements
- Author byline required
- Legal references with specific articles de loi (Code civil, loi Alur)
- Specific numbers/data points (délais, montants, pourcentages)
- Internal links to glossary terms (hover definitions)
- Sources cited (service-public.fr, legifrance)

### Internal Links (minimum 3)
- [ ] Link to glossary/[term] for each legal term
- [ ] Link to outils/[calculator] where applicable
- [ ] Link to templates/[template] where applicable
- [ ] Link to related blog articles

### OG Image
- Path: /api/og?title=[article-title]&category=[category]
- Format: 1200x630px
```
